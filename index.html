Patient Monitoring

Research into techniques for patient monitoring, by Peter H. Charlton

The research I am involved in focuses on physiological monitoring of hospital patients, divided into three areas: techniques to estimate clinical parameters from physiological signals; technologies for continuous and intermittent patient monitoring in ambulatory and intensive care settings; and, techniques for early warning of clinical deteriorations.

GitHub Repositories

The Respiratory Rate Estimation project aims to assess methods for automated respiratory rate (RR) monitoring of hospital patients. It consists of a series of studies of different algorithms for RR estimation from clinical data, complimented by the provision of publicly available datasets and resources.
