Patient Monitoring

Research into techniques for patient monitoring ~ Peter H. Charlton

The research I am involved in focuses on physiological monitoring of hospital patients, divided into three areas: signal processing techniques to estimate clinical parameters from physiological signals; equipment for continuous and intermittent patient monitoring in ambulatory and intensive care settings; and, techniques for early warning of clinical deteriorations.
